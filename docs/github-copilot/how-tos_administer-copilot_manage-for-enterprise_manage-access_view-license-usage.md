# Viewing Copilot license usage in your enterprise

View how many users have access to GitHub Copilot across your enterprise.

## Who can use this feature?

Enterprise owners

Enterprises with a Copilot Enterprise or Copilot Business plan

Copy as Markdown

If you have a Copilot Business or Copilot Enterprise plan, you can view usage information for Copilot in your enterprise. This information includes the number of seats assigned and the total spending for the current billing cycle.

1.  Navigate to your enterprise. For example, from the [Enterprises](https://github.com/settings/enterprises?ref_product=ghec&ref_type=engagement&ref_style=text) page on GitHub.com.
    
2.  At the top of the page, click **Billing and licensing**.
    
3.  Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the **Copilot** tab.
