# Upgrading GitHub Copilot for your enterprise

Change your organizations' plans from Copilot Business to Copilot Enterprise.

## Who can use this feature?

Enterprise owners

Enterprises with a Copilot Business plan

## In this article

Note

You must already have a paid Copilot Business plan to upgrade to Copilot Enterprise. Trial plans are not eligible for upgrade.

1.  Navigate to your enterprise. For example, from the [Enterprises](https://github.com/settings/enterprises?ref_product=ghec&ref_type=engagement&ref_style=text) page on GitHub.com.
2.  At the top of the page, click **Billing and licensing**.
3.  In the "Billing and licensing" sidebar, click **Licensing**.
4.  In the "Copilot" section, click **Manage**.
5.  Click the **Organizations** tab.
6.  Locate the organization for which you want to upgrade Copilot.
7.  To the right of the organization name, select the **Copilot** dropdown menu, and click **Copilot Enterprise**.

## [Troubleshooting plan migrations](#troubleshooting-plan-migrations)

When transitioning between different Copilot plans, you may encounter situations that require support.

### [Trial expiration notices](#trial-expiration-notices)

If you see a trial expiration notice but have an active paid subscription, this is a display issue and does not affect your access. If this persists, contact [GitHub Support](https://support.github.com).

### [Migrations requiring support assistance](#migrations-requiring-support-assistance)

Some migrations cannot be completed through self-service options.

-   **Changing between Copilot Business and Copilot Enterprise**: If you need to migrate between these plans within your enterprise and the option isn't available in your "Billing & Licensing" settings, contact [GitHub Support](https://support.github.com) or your account manager for assistance.
-   **Non-enterprise to enterprise environment migrations**: When moving from standalone Copilot plans to a GitHub Enterprise environment, contact [GitHub Support](https://support.github.com) or your account manager for assistance to avoid service interruption.
